### Who We Are

**ASHR Group** is a proudly Nigerian diversified company delivering trusted solutions across four key industries:

- **ASHR Run and Drive** – Your trusted partner for premium cars, SUVs and fleet vehicles
- **ASHR Designs** – Beautiful, modern interiors that transform homes and offices
- **High Taste Ceramics** – Luxury tiles, sanitary wares and bathroom fittings
- **Simtex Manhole Cover** – Strong, safe and durable FRP manhole & gully covers for roads and estates

From the cars you drive to the homes you live in and the infrastructure that supports our cities — we are committed to quality, innovation and long-term value.
